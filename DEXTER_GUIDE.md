## 🏗️ How Dexter Thinks (The 3-Layer Brain)

Dexter uses a sophisticated reasoning system to ensure every trade is high-probability:

1. **Layer 1: The Detective (Observation)**
   Dexter scans multiple timeframes (from 1-minute to Daily charts) to map the market structure. He looks for **Liquidity Sweeps**—moments where big banks "trap" small traders at session highs or lows (Asia, London, NY).

2. **Layer 2: The Red Team (Self-Critique)**
   Before any trade is taken, Dexter’s brain splits. A "Head of Research" persona critiques the initial idea, looking for hidden risks like high volatility or "Bull Traps." If the Red Team doesn't rate the trade a **7/10 or higher**, the trade is cancelled.

3. **Layer 3: The Guardian (Safety & Learning)**
   Dexter checks your real-time legal limits (like the 3-day-trade rule on Schwab) and handles all the math. Every evening, he reviews his performance to learn exactly why he was right or wrong, getting smarter every single day.

Dexter is specialized in a professional strategy used by hedge funds:
